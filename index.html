Take advantage of an ideal satellite internet service

The VSAT network service is a high-speed satellite internet service between our customers and our internet backbone intended for both private and public customers. This service is one of the most stable services, with an SLA of 99.9% availability. Our VSAT platform uses a 7.3m/100W antenna at BSC teleport and uses an iDirect 20 slot Hub. Different modems are required at the customer premises to operate on these various networks. Customer terminals vary in different sizes.
